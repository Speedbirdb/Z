The World of Enterprise Computing Technologies

Pioneered by IBM represented as IBM Z

For over a century, the pursuit of progress has driven the world’s greatest technological advancements, shaping industries and economies. In this journey, IBM has stood as a beacon of innovation, transforming how we process and utilize information. From pioneering punch card systems to breakthroughs in artificial intelligence and hybrid cloud computing, IBM has continually evolved to meet the challenges of a changing world.

IBM mainframes, especially the IBM Z series, are vital across various industries due to their reliability and ability to handle large transaction volumes. They process up to one trillion transactions daily and are used by about 71% of Fortune 500 companies. Mainframes manage 90% of credit card transactions and account for 68% of global IT workloads while representing only 6% of total IT costs
